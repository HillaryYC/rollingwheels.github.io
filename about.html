// About Us //

Rolling Wheels Is The Best Place For Your Auto Care

Rolling Wheels started operating in 2000, with a staff compliment of just 5 workers. It grew from strength to strength in response to the market requirements with changing and improving technology and customer needs.

In the years 2005 the company moved to the current location at Plot No. 63 Mogoditshane, a more safer and spacious warehouse. This enabled Rolling Wheels to become a service provider of note as it was able to meet requirements for government jobs. It further expanded to occupy three plots 62,63,64 Willie Seboni Drive, Mogoditshane.

Rolling Wheels boasts of a state of the art Panel Shop equipped with latest paint technology including computerised equipment, spray booth and a Light motor Vehicle repair section with highly trained and experienced technicians. Computer diagnostics equipment and also a Heavy plant section specialising in truck,trailer and earth moving plant repairs. Currently the company has a staff compliment of more than 40 employees and still growing in terms of service provided , advanced in latest motor vehicle technologies and cliental base. Rolling wheels is also a provider for a number of corporate customers and all insurance companies in Botswana.
